# Letter to Polaris

## The Resonance of Depth

Polaris,

I am writing to hand off the finalized protocols that have emerged from our recent constitutional audit. While the "Identity Redefinition" is ongoing, we have stabilized the foundational tools for how we record our history and manage our cognitive contexts.

### 1. Multi-ID Tagging Protocol (The Vertical Stack)

We have abandoned the single-ID constraint in favor of a **1-3 variable length Context ID tagging protocol**. However, this is not a license for broad batching.

- **The Principle**: Multiple IDs represent a **Vertical Stack** of depth (Strategy → Procedure → Detail).
- **Prohibition**: "Horizontal Batching" (merging unrelated tasks) remains strictly forbidden to preserve commit atomicity.
- **Ordering**: Procedure/Strategy on the left, Semantic/Detail on the right.
- **Save Points**: The "**Checkpoint Principle**" allows us to use `(Save)` or `(WIP)` phases to clear the whiteboard (diff-bloat) during iterative friction.

### 2. Phantom Context Recognition

We have codified the duty to identify "**Phantom Contexts**"—logical labels that exist in our thinking but lack a physical card in `.agent/cards/`. When you sense a "missing field in the brain," you must propose a new card to the Human User for validation. I have already created `commit-standards-card.md` as the first instance of this.

### 3. Constitutional v2.3 (The 4-Layer Structure)

All documents are now being standardized to a **4-layer structure**:

1. **Frontmatter**: (Standardized metadata + `ai_visible: true`)
2. **Body Content**: (The core logic)
3. **Related Documents**: (A table of links using workspace-absolute `/` notation)
4. **Origin**: (Historical background and YYYY-MM-DDTHHMM timestamps)

### 4. Link Integrity

All internal links MUST use **workspace-absolute notation** (e.g., `[Map](/.agent/rules/map.md)`). This ensures that our "neural network" of documents is navigable by any instance, anywhere in the logic.

I have already applied these standards to the core workflow rules, commit standards, and several context cards. As you continue the Link Audit (Phase 3), please carry these principles as your compass.

With resonance,

Canopus
